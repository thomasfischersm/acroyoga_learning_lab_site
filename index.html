About Acroyoga

Acroyoga is very playful in approach and vigorously intense exercise.

Usually, a trio of three plays together: The base is the person on the ground lifting the flyer into the air. The spotter shadows the moves to safely guide the flyer down in case of a crash.

Think of being a kid and climbing in an apple tree. You are simply exploring. There is no right way. However, to read the juiciest apple, it’s going to take you physically all that you’ve got. Acro is kind of like that.

Many people quickly realize that acro is also a lot about trust and communication. You are going to learn to speak up and communicate really quickly to avoid crashing. It’s also a rare experience of allowing yourself to depend on someone else and to have that trust rewarded.

About Learning Labs

We had a realization that group classes can sometimes be a struggle. You try to copy the instructor demo with another beginner. You don’t know who makes the mistake because you are probably both making mistakes. You are told that you miss foundations from another class. However, the classes you keep going to keep teaching you stuff you already know.

When you first walk into a learning lab, you are paired with a more advanced student. That student teaches you the first mini-lesson hands-on. You get to feel what it should feel like. You get guided by someone who knows. Once you know a mini-lesson, you can teach it to the next person coming.

You learn a mini-lesson from a more advanced student. Then you teach a mini-lesson to a more beginning student. A progressive curriculum is structured for this collaborative learning. An instructor is available for questions and jumps in for hands-on coaching.
