What is acroyoga?

AcroYoga is a physical practice that combines elements of yoga, acrobatics, and healing arts. It involves practicing yoga poses and acrobatic moves with a partner, often while one person is supporting the other in the air. AcroYoga can be practiced by people of all ages and skill levels and is typically taught in a class setting by a certified AcroYoga instructor. The practice is known for its ability to improve strength, flexibility, coordination, and overall physical fitness, as well as for its potential to reduce stress, improve mental well-being, and build confidence. It is also often described as a fun and enjoyable activity that can foster a sense of community and connection with others. It is also known as partner acrobatics.
